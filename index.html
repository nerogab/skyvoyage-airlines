Our Mission

SkyVoyage Airlines was founded in 1985 with a simple mission: to make world-class aviation accessible to everyone. Starting with just three routes across West Africa, we have grown into a global carrier connecting 200 destinations across 6 continents.

Our commitment to safety, innovation, and exceptional customer experience has earned us multiple international awards including Best Airline in Africa and the prestigious Skytrax 5-Star rating — a distinction held by fewer than 10 airlines globally.
